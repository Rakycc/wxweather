Key Benefits

 

Help cover up to 80% the cost of books, tuition, housing with a flexible terms from 1 to 10 years.

Borrow up to 200000 P.A.

Support CNY, USD, GBP, JPY, HKD, CAD, EUR

What you should know as a first time borrower

 

Interest Rate

Interest rate = fixed rates + variable rates

See the rate today.

 

 

Guarantee

The loan can be guaranteed by mortgage, pledge, third party guarantee, insurance and many other means.

 

 

Loan Currency

RMB, USD, Japanese Yen, Euro, British Pound, Hong Kong Dollar, Australian Dollar and Canadian Dollar.

 

 

Loan origination

Once your loan is approved, you can choose the mode of payment. Contact the nearest BOC branch for further information.

Find the nearest branch

 

 

Purpose

The loan can be used for tuition and miscellaneous fees, transportation fees, living expenses and overseas study security deposit for borrowers or their immediate family members and legal guardians to study abroad.

 

 

Term

Terms of the loan of tuition and miscellaneous fees, transportation fees, living expenses shall be ten years at most (including ten years). The term of the overseas study deposit shall not exceed the borrower's term of study.

 

 

Maximum Limit of Loan

The maximum amount: 80% of the total amount of the borrowers overseas study; the maximum amount of living expenses and transportation fees: 120,000 - 200,000 RMB per year; the maximum amount of the security deposit: 1.5 million RMB.

 

 

Repayment terms

You can repay the loan through remittance or online-banking. You can also entrust any other person to repay the loan. Repay the currency you borrowed. CNY will not be accepted for foreign currency loans.


 

 

Repayment Grace Period

The student loan offers you a grace period of up to two years. Be sure to finish your payment before you graduate or leave school. During this period, you only need to pay the interest. Repayment begins when the grace period expires.

 

 

Documents Required

**All the documents below need originals and photocopies**

For Parents or Sponsors

Identity card

Proof of work

Proof of income

Authorization documents to allow the bank to inquire about their credit reports

Proof of mortgage/pledge objects (as for mortgage/pledge)

Written consent (as for mortgage/pledge)

Valuation report (as for mortgage)

Rights certificate (as for pledge)

For Students

Identity Card

Enrolled letter

Proof of tuition fees and estimated living expenses



Frequently Asked Questions

 

Who's eligible?

To qualify for a Student Loan, you need to:

o Be a Canadian citizen or permanent resident.

o International students can apply with a creditworthy C.A. citizen or permanent resident co-signer.

o Have good credit or have a qualified co-signer.

o Have no prior student loan defaults.

o Be at least 18 years of age and have attained the age of majority in your state of residence.

How do I apply?

You need to complete your application and provide the following information:

o Student's graduation date, loan period and the loan amount.

o Student's name, date of birth and Social Security Number.

o Name of school the student is attending.

o Cost of attendance and any financial aid received (see award letter from the school).

Will this loan application impact my credit score?

The Bank of China Student Loan are credit-based loans which will be seen as an inquiry on the applicant's credit report.

How will my loan funds be sent?

We'll disburse your loan funds directly to the school.
